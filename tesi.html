Thesis Project Summary

The thesis focuses on the design of Design Transparency Labels to make interactions with AI-driven interfaces—especially within social media—more understandable and transparent to users.

The work integrates theoretical analysis and hands-on experimentation through co-design sessions, with the goal of developing visual and textual solutions that promote transparency, comprehension, and digital wellbeing on digital platforms.

Research Objectives

Scientific Objectives

  • Map AI-driven dark patterns.
  • Analyze how and when AI influences user engagement.
  • Study transparency, user control, and digital wellbeing.

Design Objectives

  • Organize co-design workshops.
  • Co-design intuitive and accessible labels.
  • Create guidelines for designers.
